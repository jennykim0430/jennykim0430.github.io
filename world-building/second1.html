By sponsoring a Dog Kennel, you are helping vulnerable dogs that arrive at our gates every day with nowhere else to turn, whatever their circumstance or history. You will be providing them with the best level of care, urgent medical treatment and safe shelter for as long as it takes until they find a new loving home.


A dog will teach you unconditional love. If you can have that in your life, things won't be too bad.

From as little as $30 a month, you could provide rescue pets with a safe and warm place to stay until they find their loving new home. Donating by text is a quick, easy way to help more rescue dogs and cats. If you would like to donate by SMS, text SAFE to 70545 to give $30 a month through your mobile.
